First-aid Instructions

Call 000 if you see someone is drowning. Do the following while waiting for the professionals.
Drowning Treatment:
  • Check for any lifeguard at beach and if any notify them. If not, inform someone to call 000 immediately.
  • If alone, take the person out of the water and check for breathing by placing your ear near to the victim's mouth and nose. Also, check for any movement of person’s chest.
  • If no sign of breathing, check person's pulse for 10 seconds.
  • If there is no Pulse, start CPR immediately.
  • Place the heel of one hand on the patient’s chest.
  • Keep your arms locked (straight).
  • Push down hard and fast 30 times. (use two hands if finding it difficult to push down with one hand)
  • Each compression should push the patient’s chest down by roughly one-third.
  • Pinch the nose of the patient closed.
  • Cover the patient's mouth with yours to create an airtight seal.
  • Blow gently and steadily into their mouth 2 times, each for 1 second.
  • Check for any reaction from the patient.
  • If patient starts responding, place them in the recovery position (lying on their side with one hand supporting their head)
  • If no response, continue compressions and breathes until further help arrives.
CPR for babies
  • Follow steps for CPR as above. However, use two fingers instead of the palm when doing chest compressions.
