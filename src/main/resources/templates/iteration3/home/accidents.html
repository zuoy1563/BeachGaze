Learn From Historic Accidents Around Beaches

Although it sounds scary that there are many accidents throughout the year, by reading the following cases, it could be understood that more often than not, accidents are actually caused by human errors. The accident rates can significantly drop if more attentions can be paid.

25 Mar 2018

Two people died around Sunshine Coast due to an accident

The bodies of two people were washed up and found on 25 Mar 2018 with their abandoned jet ski. The police said it is pretty common for people to park their jet skis around and go to swim in the surf there.

2 March 2017

A man died after he was in trouble with his sons in the water

A man died at a beach in NSW. At the time, he was swimming with his two sons, who are aged 11 and 14. Suddenly, they all got into trouble in the water. The two boys were rescued successfully, while the man was pulled out from the water unresponsively and died at the scene.
