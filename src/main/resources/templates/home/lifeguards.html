Volunteer Lifeguard Services Timetable

According to Life Saving Victoria, the largest provider of professional lifeguards in Australia - the Australian Lifeguard Service, their volunteer lifesavers are on duty from 12:00 - 17:00 during the period from the following dates.

Note: they only provide patrols on weekends and most public holidays during the period. If you cannot see the red and yellow flags, it indicates that the lifesavers are not on duty, then please do not let your children swim around. ALWAYS swim only between te red and yellow flags at a patrolled beach!
