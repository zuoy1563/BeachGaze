Water Quality

Sea water quality is an important indicator for you to decide if the beach is suitable for you to take your children to swim or not. The virtualised water quality by location is shown in the following graph. If a point has value of 0, it does not mean that there is no bacteria in the sea water, but there is only few bacteria so that it cannot be observed clearly.

It is obvious that at most of time, the quality of sea water is good. However, there are some extremely big numbers shown in the historical data as well at some beaches. So it is significant to check the current water quality before your journey.

Shark Attack Cases

Some parents may concern about shark attacks around beaches. Reports show that there are approximately 23 people died from shark attacks each year in Australia, but only about 2 cases are in Victoria, which means Victorian beaches are relatively safe.
